Why Web Dev?

I graduated from East Carolina University with a B.S. in Kinesiology. Originally, I had my sight set on Biomechanics. I decided that I would work in a corporate environment within my scope (Corporate Health). With this, I wanted to build a strong foundation of professional skills before pursuing my Masters in Biomechanics.

Eventually, I landed at NetApp, and did not realize at the time how this specific company would change the trajectory of my career. I learned the things that I set out to learn. Building projects from the ground up, the documentation/revisions that went with that. All of the different departments that I had to maintain strong relationships with, meeting-culture, and the strict standards that a corporate environment holds one to.

But even more importantly, I built a very strong rapport with Software Developers. This is where my career goals changed.
